About Us

My name is Susie, I am the owner of Stellar. In 2015, I started using CBD oil and enjoyed its benefits. I then created my own perfect body butter recipe that my Mom and I were using daily As we spread the word about this incredible concoction, friends and family became recipients of a special little jar of body butter.

Sequim Hemp Company is based in the beautiful Pacific Northwest town of Sequim, Washington. Sequim has the waters of Strait of Juan de Fuca and Victoria, BC to the North and the Olympic National Forest to the South.
